Pad Thai

Description

Pad thai is one of my favorite dishes to make. After trying many different recipes over the years, I have finally stumbled the one that is by far the best. I've tweaked it a bit and have come up with a nine-step dish that is layered, authentic, and affordable!

Ingredients

  • Produce: red bell pepper, jalapeno, turnip, serrano pepper, garlic, scallions, bean sprouts, cilantro, limes.
  • Pad thai noodles, fish sauce, rice vinegar, salt, sugar, brown sugar, Sambal Oelek, sweet rice dried chiles, flour, bread crumbs.
  • Tofu, eggs.

Steps

  1. Cut red bell pepper.
  2. Rehydrate noodles with hot water.
  3. Fish sauce and jalapeno bath.
  4. Pickle turnips, serrano peppers, and jalapenos in rice vinegar, salt, and sugar.
  5. Pad thai sauce: rice vinegar, Sambal Oelek, brown sugar, and fish sauce.
  6. Toppings: Toasted sweet rice, toasted garlic, crushed dried chiles, fried scallions and jalapenos.
  7. Tofu, egg, scallion, flour, fish sauce, bread crumbs. Fry in hot oil.
  8. Wok: oil, garlic, red bell pepper, scallion, bean sprouts, noodles, sauce, eggs, pickle.
  9. Assembly: Pad thai, toppings, jalapenos in fish sauce, bean sprouts, cilantro, pad thai sauce, lime.
