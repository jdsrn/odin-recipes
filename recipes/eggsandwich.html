Scrambled Egg Sandwich with Tomato Jam

Description

The tomato jam is really what takes this sandwich to the next level. I've made this for myself and even for a couple of friends. Food is meant to be shared right? DM me to find out which Instrammer inspired me to try this recipe out!

Ingredients

  • Dave's Killer Bread, egg (2 per person), avocado (1/2 per person), grated parmesan, aged white cheddar or smoked gouda.
  • Tomato jam: 2 cups cherry tomatoes, 2 garlic cloves, 2 tbsp brown sugar, 2 tbsp fresh lime juice, 1/2 shallot, 1 tsp cumin, 1/4 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper.
